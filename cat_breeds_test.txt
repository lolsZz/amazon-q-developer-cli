Top 5 Most Common Cat Breeds
================================

1. Domestic Shorthair
   - Mixed breed cat with short coat
   - Most common household cat
   - Variety of colors and patterns

2. Persian
   - Long-haired breed with flat face
   - Requires regular grooming
   - Calm and gentle temperament

3. Maine Coon
   - Large, long-haired breed
   - Known for their size and friendly nature
   - Originally from Maine, USA

4. Siamese
   - Short-haired with distinctive color points
   - Vocal and social personality
   - Blue eyes and slender build

5. Ragdoll
   - Large, semi-long-haired breed
   - Known for going limp when picked up
   - Blue eyes and docile temperament
